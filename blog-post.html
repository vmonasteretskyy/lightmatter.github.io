Learn how to get access to the private alpha of CryptoWars!

MAY 14, 2019 reviews

As you may have already heard, CryptoWars is our newest partner on the space!

What is CryptoWars?

CryptoWars is a completely decentralized blockchain Real-Time Strategy (RTS) game. Build a village, hire troops and fight enemies! One of the most important thing is that items and skins can be easily sold or transferred to anyone, offering you the possibility to even make profits.

Experimental — the CryptoWars creators  will be building their game using the Loom SDK. That means that assets, status, logic and others will be stored on a dedicated blockchain. Read more about the Loom implementation here.

How to start playing?

CryptoWars officially launches on Dec 1st. 2018. But there is a way to get in earlier! They have recently launched a marketplace allowing you to buy assets. Acquire any skin or starter pack (minimum 0.1 ETH) and have the chance to build your village earlier than anyone else on the private alpha!
